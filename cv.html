Tom gets invited to Taster Day at Hutch Games

I was excited to be invited to the Mini Taster Day at Hutch Games. I'm interested in gaming as I've always thought that it is at the cutting edge of software development. I now understand why! I wanted to create a game ahead of this event so I could appreciate the environments their Development team works in. Tom's new gaming business generates over £2,000

May 2022

At the start of 2020, I began playing a video game called CS:GO. I noticed that a lot of people had expensive skins and I realised I could capitalise on this so that is exactly what I did. In the beginning, I deposited £20 into my steam wallet and traded it up to £35. This made me see the potential of the CS:GO skin market. I kept on depositing, buying, selling, and trading for profit. I ended up making more than £2,000 and still counting my current inventory value is £1,700. I've enjoyed the process and met a lot of good friends whilst doing this.
