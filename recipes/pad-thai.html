Pad Thai

(For my own reference)

Ingredients

Sauce

  • 1 1/2 tsp tamarind paste / concentrate
  • 1/3 cup coconut aminos / low salt soy sauce or 1/5 cup soy sauce
  • 3 tbsp palm sugar / coconut sugar
  • 1 1/2 chili garlic sauce
  • 1 1/2 tbsp lime juice
  • 2 tsp fish sauce

Pad Thai

  • 200g tofu / prawns / chicken
  • 2 Thai red chiles or 1/2 tsp chili flakes
  • 1 tbsp grated/crushed garlic
  • 1 tbsp coconut aminos / soy sauce
  • 1 cup bean sprouts
  • 1 chopped spring onions
  • 1/3 cup roasted salted peanuts
  • 225g rice stick noodles (around 3/5 a standard pack)
  • 2 tbsp vegetable or alternative oil
  • 2 eggs

Recipe

  1. Add all sauce ingredients to a small saucepan and simmer for 30 secs whilest stiring. Then set aside.
  2. Add noodles to large bowl and cover with boiling water. Cook acording to package instructions (usally 6 mins).
  3. Drain noodles and toss with 1 tbsp oil.
  4. Fry tofu / prawns / chicken untill it browns.
  5. Add eggs into pan and cook untill scrambled.
  6. Add chiles / chili flakes with garlic and coconut aminos / soy sauce.
  7. Cook for 3 more mins then add noodles, bean sprouts, spring onions, peanuts and pad thai sauce.
  8. Toss for 3 mins and serve.
