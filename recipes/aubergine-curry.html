Aubergine Curry

(For my own reference)

Ingredients

Roasted Aubergine

  • 2 Medium aubergine
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp pepper
  • 2 tbsp vegetable or alternative oil

Curry

  • 3 tbsp coconut milk
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1 1/4 cups water
  • 1 tbsp grated/crushed ginger
  • 1 tbsp grated/crushed garlic
  • 1 - 2 diced tomatos or 3 tbsp canned tomato/passata
  • 2 red shallots or 1 red onion
  • 10-15 curry leaves (preferably fresh)
  • 1 tsp black mustard seeds
  • 3 tbsp vegetable or alternative oil

Spice Mix

  • 1/4 tsp cardamon powder
  • 1/4 tsp ground clove
  • 1/4 tsp black pepper
  • 1/2 tsp south asian chili powder (use kashmire for less spice)
  • 4 tsp coriander powder
  • 4 tsp cumin powder

Recipe

  1. Preheat oven to 240°C / 220°C fan.
  2. Cut aubergine in 1" x 1" x 4-5" pieces.
  3. Place cut aubergine in bowl and toss with salt, pepper and oil.
  4. Spread aubergine on tray and roast for 20 mins, turn and roast for a further 10 mins. Should be soft and browned.
  5. Once you turn aubergine in the oven start making the curry. Heat oil in saucepan, add mustard seeds and let them sizzle for 15 seconds.
  6. Add curry leaves, stir and leave to sizzle for a further 15 seconds.
  7. Add onion and cook for 5 minutes untill golden brown.
  8. Add tomato cook for 1 minute whilest stiring.
  9. Add garlic and ginger and cook for 2 minutes whilest stiring.
  10. Turn heat down to low and add spice mix whilest stiring.
  11. Stir in water and roasted Aubergine and reduce to simmer. Stiring occasionally cook for 30 mins, add more water if dries out.
  12. Stir in coconut milk, add salt to taste and serve.
