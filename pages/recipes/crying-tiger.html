Crying Tiger

(For my own reference)

Ingredients

Steak

  • 1/2 tbsp oyster sauce
  • 1/2 tbsp fish sauce
  • 1/2 tbsp dark soy sauce
  • 1/2 tsp palm sugar/coconut sugar
  • 2 tbsp vegetable or alternative oil
  • 200g ribeye/sirlion steak

Nahm Jim Jaew

  • 1 tsp jasmine or sticky rice
  • 1 tbsp tamarind paste/concentrate
  • 1 1/2 tbsp fish sauce
  • 1 tbsp lime juice
  • 1/2 tbsp palm sugar/coconut sugar
  • 3 Thai red chilis or 1 tsp Thai chili flakes
  • 1/2 red shallot/red onion
  • 1 tsp coriander

Recipe

  1. Mix oyster sauce, fish sauce, soy sauce and palm suger together.
  2. Massage marinade into steak, put into zip lock, remove all air and let marinad for 4 - 24 hours.
  3. Toast rice in pan untill golden brown and crush in pestal and morter untill powder.
  4. Dice shallot and corriander and mix with rice powder, tamarind concentrate, fish sauce, lime juice, palm sugar and chili and mix.
  5. Heat pan untill it nearly smokes and add oil. Let oil heat untill nearly it nearly smokes then add marinated steak and cook for 2-3 mins each side or untill charred.
  6. Let steak rest for 5 mins and serve with nahm jim jaew.
