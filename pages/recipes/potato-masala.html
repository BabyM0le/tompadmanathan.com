Potato Masala

(For my own reference)

Ingredients

Masala

  • 4 medium potatos
  • 2 diced tomatos
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric
  • 1 tsp jerra (cumin seeds)
  • 1 tsp mustard seeds
  • 4 cloves garlic crushed
  • 3 tbsp vegetable or alternative oil
  • 1 large red onion
  • 1 tsp coriander powder
  • 1 tbsp kashmir chili powder
  • 2 thin green chiles split
  • 2 cups water
  • 6-8 curry leaves (preferably fresh)

Recipe

  1. Peel and chop potato into 1 inch cubes then boil for 10 mins and set aside.
  2. Heat oil in pan, once hot add mustard seeds, jerra and curry leaves. Cook untill mustard seeds splatter.
  3. Add garlic and cook for 1 min.
  4. Add onion and green chili and cook for 3-4 mins.
  5. Add tomatos, turmeric, coriander and chili powder. Then saute untill tomatos are softened.
  6. Add cooked potatos, water and cover and cook for 10 mins. Stiring every few minutes.
