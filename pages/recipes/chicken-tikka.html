Chicken Tikka

(For my own reference)

Ingredients

Marinade

  • 300g plain full fat greek yogurt
  • 4 crushed cloves garlic
  • 2 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp turmeric
  • 2 tbsp south asian chili powder (use kashmire for less spice)
  • 2 tbsp garam masala
  • 1 tsp cumin
  • 1/2 tsp black pepper
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice

Chicken

  • 1 kg chicken thigh
  • 2 tbsp oil

Recipe

  1. Mix all marinade ingredients in large bowl. Add chicken (diced or whole) and ensure all surfices of chicken is covered. Leave in fridge for 16 - 36 hours.
  2. Preheat grill to maximum temperature for minimum 20mins. Make sure not to open the oven unless taking out or putting in oven tray.
  3. Lay chicken on oven rack and put in oven for around 10mins or untill charded, then turn and brush with oil.
  4. Take out of oven and let rest on rack for 10mins.
