Rasam Powder

(For my own reference)

Ingredients

Spices

  • 1/2 tbsp toor dal
  • 6 fenugreek seeds
  • 1 tsp coriander seeds
  • 1 tsp jerra (cumin) seeds
  • 1 tsp peppercorn
  • 2 dried red chili
  • 6-8 curry leaves (preferably fresh)
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • 1/5 tsp asafoetida powder

Recipe

  1. Toast all spices in a pan on medium-low heat for 5mins or untill fragrant and golden brown.
  2. Leave spices to cool untill room temperature.
  3. Grind spices in pestle and mortar, spice grinder or blender until a course powder.
