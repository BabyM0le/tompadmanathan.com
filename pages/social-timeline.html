Social Timeline

Tom launches his first website

July 2021

As my interview at WiSH was fast approaching, I decided to make a CV website to test my skills and demonstrate my creativity. This was a challenging task as I had never done anything similar to this before, so I was just learning as I went along. After about a week of development, I finally launched it on a free subdomain on my personal computer. The Tom Padmanathan V1 website can be viewed Here.

Tom recieves math award from Saint Cecilias

September 2020

After my first year at Saint Cecilias (Year 7), I received an award for the highest score in Mathematics in my year (150 students). Only a few years prior, I needed extra tuition in this subject. It was great relief to see my hard work paying off and what I can achieve if I put my mind to a task. Two and a half years later I received a best improvement in the year in Science.

Tom gets his turtles

September 2019

I have always been fascinated by different exotic animals and in 2019 my dream of owning one became true. For my birthday, my Dad said I could finally get some pet turtles, after a two-hour drive we collected them, the tank, and the fish and brought them home. I named them: Kiki and Zen. They will live until i'm 56 years old!

Cycling challenge - 200miles, 5 days, 3 countries...

August 2019

In 2019, I set off on a 5 day cycling challenge to Lake Constance. We cycled 200 miles in five days across Germany, Austria and Switzerland. This was my 'Comming of Age' trip for my thirteenth birthday.

Tom builds his first computer

May 2019

All my friends at school had a gaming machine whether it be a: console, laptop, or PC. I thought I was missing out, so I did some research into it and decided to build my very first computer. After ordering the parts from Amazon, my friend Ben and I spent a few hours assembling it and setting it up and it worked! This kickstarted my career and hobbies in technology.

Tom performs 'My Music' at Saint Cecilias

February 2019

Similar to other things, music is something I was never naturally talented in and had to learn through hard work and patience. After a short period of learning the guitar, I wrote and performed 'My Music' at Saint Cecilias music scholarship program. I was sad to hear I was 2 points away from a music scholarship offer, however, it was still an amazing experience and I hope to improve my guitar in the future.

Tom sits SATS exam

June 2018

After two years of preparation, it was time to sit for my SATS. This was my first major examination and I was nervous. I was happy to find out my results: 91% English Reading, 92% SPAG, and 94% in Mathematics. It would be an understatement to say I was relieved.

Tom completes London-Brighton race for the first time

June 2017

When I was growing up, every year my Dad always participated in the London to Brighton cycle race, and one day I got to experience that with him. We left at 6am. After 8 hours, 56miles, and a lot of superfoods we finally made it and I enjoyed it. I completed the challenge an aditional three times.

Tom wins Wimbledon Park Leggit race

May 2018

In 2018 the entire Wimbledon Park Primary School (600 students) competed in a cycling race around Richmond Park (7.5miles). Once we set off I was in the lead, I kept my position for most of the race until I broke suddenly and my chain came off my bike. I quickly stopped to put it back on but I had already lost my lead, with only one mile to go I pulled into the lead and won the event. It was a very fun event and I ended up going around 2 more times. This lit up my love for cycling even more.

Tom joins local snooker club

February 2017

I joined my local snooker club to improve my game and have some fun. I also joined a table tennis club with my friends for a few years, it was really enjoyable and a good way to spend time together.

Tom gains cooking experience in commercial kitchen

February 2017

I was delighted to hear that an Italian Restaurant was allowing me to learn to cook professionally. Once I got there I first learned all the techniques and ways to make arrabbiata, then later moved on to make pizza in an authentic wood-fired pizza oven. I still used the recipe they taught me and am forever grateful to the family-run restaurant for giving me this experience.

Tom captains under 8s rugby team to the final

April 2015

The Wimbledon under 8s rugby team went up to Northhampton to compete in their Rugby tournament. After a few matches, we ending up playing in the Semi-Final, which I captained. We went on to win that game.

Tom completes mile a day running challenge

February 2012

My Dad had an idea to keep my brother and I fit and active. Run a mile a day for an entire year. After the first few months, I realised how difficult this challenge was since there were no exceptions. Even on holiday! I'm sad to say that after 6 months I decided to stop however it still was an achievement for a 6-year-old and a great experience.

Tom completes the David Lloyd swimming challenge

October 2010

At the young age of 4 years, I completed the David Lloyd swimming challenge with my brother, which involved swimming 52 Lengths of the 25m pool. After swimming 0.8miles, I was exhausted!
