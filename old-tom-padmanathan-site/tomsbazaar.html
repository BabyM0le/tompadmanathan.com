Tom's Bazaar

Background

When I was six I went to Sri-Lanka and bought £14 worth of pepper then when I came back to England sold it and made a profit of £13. A year later, I learned to make animal balloons, bracelets and origami and sold these at car boot sales together with toys I bought from China and Sri-Lanka. Tom’s Bazaar had stalls at Primary School fairs and organized children’s parties. From 2017, I had stalls at school fairs, serviced birthday parties and also had one corporate customer. We also created a logo, branding, a website and templates for proposals and enabled electronic payments. The output of this can be seen at Tom's Bazaar's website.
